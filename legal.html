Privacy policy

Data we collect

This site collects no personal data by default. We do not use any analytics, advertising, tracking pixels, third-party cookies or fingerprinting techniques.

Geolocation (opt-in only)

The « Find a theater near you » button uses your browser's geolocation API to display nearby cinemas on a map.

  • Geolocation is requested only after you click the button.
  • Your browser explicitly asks for your permission ; you can refuse.
  • Your coordinates are used only on your device to center the map ; they are never sent to a server, never stored, never shared.
  • Cinema data comes from a static dataset bundled with the site (medias/cinema.json) and the map tiles are served by OpenStreetMap (their privacy policy applies for tile requests).

Cookies & local storage

This site sets no cookies and uses no localStorage or sessionStorage. Your browser may keep a standard HTTP cache of static assets (images, fonts, CSS) for faster repeat visits — clearable via your browser settings (Storage → Clear site data).

Third-party content

  • YouTube : the official trailer iframe is loaded from youtube-nocookie.com, YouTube's privacy-enhanced embed domain. No cookies are set until you actively interact with the player (play, pause, share). Once you interact, YouTube may set its own cookies — see the Google Privacy Policy.
  • OpenStreetMap tiles : loaded only when you click « Find a theater near you ». See OSMF Privacy Policy.
  • Fonts : self-hosted on this domain. No request is made to Google Fonts ; your IP address is never shared with Google for font loading.

Your rights (GDPR)

Since no personal data is collected or stored, requests for access, rectification or erasure are not applicable. For any question, contact contact@melinaterrier.fr.
